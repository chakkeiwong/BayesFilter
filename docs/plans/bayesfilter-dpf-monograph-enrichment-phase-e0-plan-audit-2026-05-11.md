# Audit: Phase E0 source and claim-audit setup plan

## Date

2026-05-11

## Scope

This audit reviews:
- `docs/plans/bayesfilter-dpf-monograph-enrichment-phase-e0-source-claim-audit-plan-2026-05-11.md`

The goal is to determine whether E0 is strong enough to govern the enrichment
round without drifting into a shallow source-gathering exercise.

## Assessment

Assessment: **approved with one strengthening**.

The phase is well designed and aligned with the master program.  In particular,
it correctly treats source and claim audit as a first-class phase rather than a
background clerical task.  It also correctly requires MCP use, chapter-level
claim inventories, source ledgers, and a gap register.

The main remaining weakness is that the plan should explicitly require a
**chapter-by-chapter priority ranking of load-bearing claims**.  Without that,
the phase could still devolve into broad coverage without sufficiently focusing
on the formulas and claims most likely to matter for later rewriting.

## Strengthening required

Add one explicit required output:

- a per-chapter **priority register** ranking claims/formulas into:
  - critical for implementation,
  - critical for target interpretation,
  - useful supporting context,
  - optional enrichment.

This will help later phases decide where derivation time and MCP audit time must
be spent first.

## Audit disposition

Proceed after adding the priority register requirement.

## Next phase justified?

Yes, once the priority register is added and then executed as part of E0.
